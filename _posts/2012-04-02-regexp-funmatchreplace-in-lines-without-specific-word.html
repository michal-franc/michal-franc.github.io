Solution

Expression

Most important part of this solution is (?!.*min) . This expression will try to look for min word in matched data and if it wont find it there whole match is discarded.

For further replacement procedure, I m specifying two matched groups that will be used to construct new Path. (?<path>.*)/(?<end>.*) This will match everything to last possible “/” and make group named path, then every character after “/” is put into end group.

Replace Expression

Replace expression uses matched groups like simple variables to construct new Path.
